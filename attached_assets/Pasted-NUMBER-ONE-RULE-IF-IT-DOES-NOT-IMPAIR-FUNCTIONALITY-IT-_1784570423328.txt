NUMBER ONE RULE: IF IT DOES NOT IMPAIR FUNCTIONALITY, IT IS NOT PSYCHOPATHOLOGICAL. IF IT IMPROVES FUNCTIONALITY, IT IS THE OPPOSITE OF PSYCHOPATHOLOGICAL. DO NOT USE "GRANDIOSITY" AS A STANDALONE CATEGORY. IF THERE IS OUTRIGHT DELUSION AND IT INVOLVES GRANDIOSITY, THEN THE GRANDIOSITY CAN BE MENTIONED IN THAT CONTEXT. BUT GRANDIOSITY (E.G. "I WROTE AN IMPECCABLE ESSAY") MAY BE ACCURATE AND MAY BE HEALTHY EGO-FUNCTIONING--AND MAY BE HYPER-FUNCTIONAL. AT THE SAME TIME, GRANDIOSITY COULD BE PSYCHOPATHOLOGICAL---BUT ONLY IF THERE IS INDEPENDENT EVIDENCE THAT IT IS CAUSATIVE OF SOME WELL-ESTABLISHED DYSFUNCTAIONTLI. AND OFTENTIMES, THE GRANDIOSITYH IS A HEALTHY RESPONSE TO SUCH DYSFUNCTIONALITY. SO I WOULD RATHER THAT "GRANDIOSITY" LARGLELYH BE DREOPPED AS A CATEGORY. SAME WITH "NARCISSISM." 2. IN WHAT OTHER WAYS IS THIS PERSON HIGHLY FUNCTIONAL? PROVIDE EXAMPLES AND QUOTES. 

3. WHAT IS THIS PERSON'S GREATEST PSYCHOLOGICAL WEAKNESS? I.E. WHAT IS THE PRIMARY RESPECT IN WHICH THIS PERSON IS DYSFUNCTIONAL? PROVIDE EXAMPLE AND QUOTE. 

4. IN WHAT OTHER WAYS IS THIS PERSON HIGHLY DYSFUNCTIONAL?

5. OF THIS PERSON'S DYSFUNCTIONALITIES, DO THEY APPEAR TO BE PERMANENT OR SPECIFIC TO A PASSING SITUATION? 

6. IF ENDURING, HOW ARE THEY CURRENTLY PRESENTING? 

7. WHAT IS THIS PERSON'S PRIMARY DEFENSE MECHANISM? 

8. IS IT FUNCTIONAL OR DYSFUNCTIONAL IN THIS CONTEXT? 

9. IF FUNCTIONAL, EXPLAIN HOW. 

10. IF DYSFUNCTIONAL, EXPLAIN HOW. 

11. WHAT OTHER DEFENSE MECHANISMS ARE PRESENT? 

12. ARE THEY FUNCTIONAL OR DYSFUNCTIONAL IN THIS CONTEXT? 

13. IF FUNCTIONAL, EXPLAIN HOW. 

14. IF DYSFUNCTIONAL, EXPLAIN HOW. 

15. DOES THE PERSON HAVE A STRONG SENSE OF SELF? OR IS THAT PERSON DEPENDENT ON OTHERS FOR A SENSE OF IDENTITY? 

16. WHAT CAREER DOES THIS PERSON LIKELY HAVE? IF NOT ENOUGH INFORMATION, FOR WHAT CAREER, IF ANY, DOES THIS PERSON'S CHARACTER-TRAITS (INCLUDING DEFENSES) MAKE THEM SUITABLE? 

17. HOW DOES THIS PERSON APPEAR TO COMPENSATE (I.E. FUNCTIONALLY ADAPT TO ADVERSITY)? 

18. HOW DOES THIS PERSON APPEAR TO DECOMPENSATE (I.E. DYSFUNCTIONALLY ADAPT TO ADVERSITY)? 

19. WITH REGARD TO THIS PERSON'S DYSFUNCTIONAL DEFENSES AND OTHER SYMPTOMS, DO THEY PRIMARILY INVOLVE INTERNAL CONFLICT? OR DO THEY PRIMARILY INVOLVE ALIENATION, ON AN EPISTEMIC (KNOWLEDGE-RELATED) LEVEL, FROM THE EXTERNAL WORLD? 

20. ELABORATE ON RESPONSE TO LAST QUESTION.
